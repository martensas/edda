\part{Runic Poetry}

\bookStart{Introduction to Runic Poetry}

Not all poetry preserved in Runic inscriptions is included here.  Magical inscriptions will be edited under Galders, the stanza from the Rök runestone (Ög 136) under Norse Heroic Poetry, and the Runic version of the \emph{Dream of the Rood} under Poetry on Christian Subjects.

Metrically the poetry is generally in \Fornyrdislag.  A few fragments from Jutland are in \Ljodahattr\ and two from Sweden are in \Drottkvett.
