\bvb Then bellowed out the battle-young king: \\
“This is no dawning from the east, nor does a dragon fly here, \\
nor here on this hall do the gables burn— \\
but here they bear forth arms, the birds sing, \\
the grey-pelt \ken{wolf} howls, the war-wood \ken{shield} dins, \\
the shield clashes against the spear.  Now shines the moon, \\
wandering beneath the heavens; now arise the woeful deeds \\
which wish to fulfill the evil of this host. \\
Yet awaken now, ye my warriors! \\
Lift up your hands, put your minds to zeal, \\
win by the spear-point, be in brave spirits!”\evb\evg

\bvb I have not learned that in a worthier manner in the fight of men \\

among that troop-fellowship, yet they held the doors.\evb\evg
