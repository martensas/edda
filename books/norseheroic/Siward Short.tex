\section{Introduction}

The \textbf{Short Lay of Siward} (\Sigurdskamma) continues the story of Siward, focusing on his death and Byrnhild’s subsequent grieving.  Despite its title it is one of the longest poems in \Regius, having around 280 long-lines.
